About Me üêæ

My name is Jake, I'm the trainer behind Mythos Canine! I have been obsessed with animals, mostly dogs, for as long as I can remember. I've been involved with training dogs and dog sports since I was 11 years old with my first dog Zoe.

As I grew that turned into a passion for helping dogs and their owners navigate their relationship together and the world around them! I've trained everything from various dog sports, to puppy foundations, reactivity and advanced working dogs. Including a fully trained assistance dog that I raised and trained for a friend of mine.

I also hold a BSc in Zoology with a specific focus on animal behaviour and canine welfare which I have just recently completed. Although education never ends!

I now live in the Coalville area with my own two dogs, Nimue the collie and Seoda the lagotto romagnolo. They absolutely keep me on my toes! My passion with my own dogs is agility, obedience and scentwork.

My training approach is science-based and welfare-focused. You won't see any "quick fixes" or gimmicks with me. Just real training, guaranteed to give you lasting results and to set you and your dog up for a happy, fulfilled life together.
